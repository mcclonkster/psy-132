What are Heuristics?

Mental Shortcuts

System 1 Thinking

Foundations

Algorithm vs. Heuristic

Algorithm

A methodical, logical rule or procedure that guarantees solving a particular problem.

Result: 100% Certain | Effort: High

Heuristic

A simple thinking strategy that allows for speed but is prone to occasional error.

Result: Probable | Effort: Low

Biases

Availability Heuristic

Estimating the likelihood of events based on their availability in memory.

"If you can easily recall it, it must be important/common."
