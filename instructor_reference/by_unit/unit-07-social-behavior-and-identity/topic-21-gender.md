### Common misconceptions

- Biological sex and gender are identical.
- Gender identity and sexual orientation are the same thing.
- Gender expression determines gender identity.
- Gender diversity is a disorder by definition.
- All gender differences are biological and fixed.
- All gender differences are purely social and biology plays no role.
- Socialization is simple indoctrination and people have no agency.
- Transgender identity is defined by distress rather than identity.
- Gender dysphoria means being transgender rather than referring to distress and impairment.
- Stigma and discrimination are minor compared with individual factors.
- One case can decisively prove a single-cause theory of gender development.
