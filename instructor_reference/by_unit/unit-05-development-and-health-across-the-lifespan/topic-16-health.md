### Common misconceptions

- Health outcomes are mostly personal responsibility and willpower.
- Stress is only a feeling and has no physiological component.
- Stress automatically causes disease in a direct one-to-one way.
- Cortisol is a simple stress meter that directly reveals stress level.
- All stress is harmful, so stress should be eliminated.
- One coping style is always best for every situation.
- Emotion-focused coping is the same as avoidance.
- Social support only helps by making people feel better.
- Biomarkers prove simple answers and strong causal claims.
- Type A personality reliably predicts heart disease as a single trait.
- Access barriers are minor compared with motivation.
